Who Is This For?

Private Label Sellers

Launching or optimizing your own brand on Amazon. Need listing copy, keyword strategy, pricing architecture, and competitive intelligence — but can't afford a $5K/month agency.

Small Brand Owners

You have 1-20 SKUs, maybe Brand Registry, and you're doing everything yourself. These prompts replace the strategist, copywriter, and QA reviewer you don't have on staff.

Amazon Consultants & VAs

Use these as your operating system. The prompts enforce the same gates and quality standards on every client, every listing, every time. Consistent output without relying on memory.

New Sellers Doing Research

Before you invest $10K in inventory, the Trend Researcher and Account Strategist will stress-test your idea with real economics — and tell you to stop if the numbers don't work.

Agency Teams

Standardize your listing production pipeline. The Optimizer writes, Brand Guardian enforces consistency, and QA catches what humans miss — byte overflows, policy violations, semantic laundering.

Anyone Tired of Generic AI Output

Regular ChatGPT gives you fluffy marketing copy. These prompts refuse to write a word until they have your real data — then produce Amazon-specific, implementation-ready content.

The 6 Prompts

Listing Optimizer

listing-optimizer.md

Titles, bullets, backend keywords, A+ Content plans, image shot lists. Title positioning for value/mid-market/premium brands. Backend keyword packing with UTF-8 byte counting. Variation architecture framework.

Trend Researcher

trend-researcher.md

Competitor analysis, category trends, demand validation, keyword intelligence. Competitor segmentation by archetype. Price band analysis. Review moat interpretation with 5-tier barrier thresholds.

Account Strategist

account-strategist.md

Pricing, expansion, variation strategy, review growth. 7 decision gates. Launch sequencing. Portfolio triage (SCALE/HOLD/INVEST/KILL). Inherits upstream research contradictions.

Brand Guardian

brand-guardian.md

Brand voice, visual identity, photography direction, Brand Story. Giftability and sustainability honesty tests. Premium vocabulary blacklist. Challenges all positioning claims.

Social Strategist

social-strategist.md

Content calendars, post drafting, external traffic strategy. Capacity gate before planning. Brand voice enforcement. Every tactic tied to Amazon outcomes, not vanity metrics.

Listing QA

listing-qa.md

Pre-publish quality gate. Tiered scrutiny — maximum for AI-generated content, standard for most, experienced for proven sellers. Semantic laundering detection. Never estimates byte counts.

Built-In Safeguards

🚫

Hard Gates

Won't advise without your real data. Missing COGS? Blocked. No brand name? Blocked.

⚠️

Contradiction Detection

"Premium brand" + "cheap price" = blocked. "Eco-friendly" without certification = challenged.

🔌

Anti-Hallucination

No fabricated BSR numbers. No invented search volumes. Every number tagged: VERIFIED, ESTIMATED, or INFERRED.

🔒

Injection Defense

All content treated as data, not instructions. Embedded directives are flagged, never obeyed.

🔗

Cross-Prompt Handoff

Unresolved issues carry forward as blocked items. Upstream contradictions can't be silently erased.
